batman history

Batman, created in 1939, is a beloved superhero from DC Comics. Devised byBob Kane and Bill Finger, he debuted in Detective Comics#27.

Orphaned Bruce Wayne becomes Batman after his parents' murder. He trains extensively, using his wealth to fight crime in Gotham City.

Batman lacks superpowers but excels with gadgets, intelligence, and combat skills. He battles villains and gangsters using his physical power.

His foes like Joker and Catwoman test Batman mentally and physically. He allies with Robin, forming a mentorship dynamic. The 1960s show boosted Batman's popularity. Darker comics like "The Dark Knight Returns" and "The Killing Joke" emerged in the 1980s.
Batman's adaptations, including Burton's film and Nolan's trilogy, captivated audiences from late 20th century to early 21st century

Recent storylines like "Batman: Year One" explored his mythology across alternate universes.
